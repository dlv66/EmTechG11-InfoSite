High Latency, Ping, or Lag


The physical distance from the servers makes your inputs delayed. People who are far from the server will be at a disadvantage compared to those who are close. Not all people have access to fast internet so there will be another increase in lag.



Coverage


At the start, only 1st world countries like the United States will be able to have at least close to reliable access to the service. It will take a long time to provide these services to other non-1st world countries like the Philippines due to the naturally slow internet and the long process of bringing the entire service to other countries.



Costs

Some people like to have their games be a one-time payment deal. Some platforms will still charge you the game's full price in addition to the monthly fee of the service (Google Stadia). You will need to have a good internet speed for this to work so the price of your internet service will also go up.
